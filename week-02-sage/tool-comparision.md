## Comparative Analysis
Out of the 3 AIs used, Gemini 2.5 Flask took the longest to respond, my first prompt almost taking a whole minute to process. However, it also gave the most detailed and professional-sounding responses. Each of its responses were highly detailed and used a very professional tone, like a college lecture from a very dedicated professor. The table it gave was filled to the brim with information, each entry being almost a paragraph.

ChatGPT on the other hand, was very quick and responded in a very casual tone, like a friend taking their time to explain something to you. The table it gave was very simple, each entry was just a few words, not even a sentence. It also asked follow-up questions to each response, asking if I wanted any extra clarification or details to help me better understand the topic, or talk about a different, related topic.

Finally, Claude was like a mixture of both AIs, being both detailed and in-depth like Gemini, but also casual and friendly like ChatGPT. It also asked follow-up questions like ChatGPT, contributing to that casual tone, while keeping up the pattern of clear and understandable answers Flask started. I think I’ll use this one.
